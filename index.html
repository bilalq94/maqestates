The Estate

Central Florida's Hidden Jewel

Indian Lake Estates is a gated private community set midway between the Atlantic Ocean and Gulf of Mexico — a 7,500-acre sanctuary surrounding Lake Walk-in-Water, Polk County's largest and most celebrated fishing lake.

Originally envisioned as Florida's most exclusive residential enclave, the community features 200+ miles of roads, a marina, country club, interfaith worship houses, dining, fitness, and world-class outdoor recreation — all within your private gates.
