Lake Walk In Water

Florida’s Most
Legendary Lake

Lake Weohyakapka — beloved as “Lake Walk In Water” — is a 7,500-acre freshwater jewel at the heart of Indian Lake Estates. Nationally celebrated as one of the finest largemouth bass fisheries in the United States, it is the crown jewel that makes this community unlike any other in Central Florida.

The longest freshwater fishing pier in all of Florida extends into the lake, complete with a pavilion, propane grills, and covered picnic tables. A private marina, boat launch, beach area, and water sports facilities complete an unrivalled waterfront lifestyle.
